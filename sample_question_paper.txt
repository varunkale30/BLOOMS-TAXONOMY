Sample Question Paper - Science Class 10

6. Design an experiment to test the effect of temperature on plant growth.
7. What are the three states of matter?

9. Apply Newton's laws to explain why a car stops when brakes are applied.
10. Analyze the relationship between population growth and resource consumption.
11. What is the capital of France?
12. Explain the water cycle in detail.
13. How would you calculate the area of a circle?
14. Compare the advantages and disadvantages of renewable and non-renewable energy sources.
15. Evaluate the effectiveness of vaccination programs in preventing disease.
16. Create a model of the solar system showing the relative positions of planets.
17. List the main components of the human digestive system.
18. Interpret the data shown in the graph below.
19. Apply the concept of density to explain why oil floats on water.
20. Analyze the factors that contribute to air pollution in urban areas.
